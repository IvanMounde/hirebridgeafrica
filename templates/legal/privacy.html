Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025 · Version 1.0

1. Data We Collect

We collect: account information (name, email, phone); CV and career documents you upload; job application data; booking and payment records; usage data (pages visited, searches performed); consent records (timestamp, IP address, version of terms accepted); and communications you send us.

2. How We Use Your Data

To provide and improve our services; process bookings and applications; send transactional and (with consent) marketing communications; comply with legal obligations; and maintain security of the Platform. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Legal Basis (Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019)

We process your data under: contractual necessity (service delivery); your explicit consent (marketing communications); legitimate interests (platform security, fraud prevention); and legal obligation (record-keeping).

4. Data Sharing

We may share your data with: employers (only when you apply for a job or book a recruitment service); trusted service providers (hosting, email delivery) under data processing agreements; and legal authorities when required by law.

5. Data Retention

We retain account data for as long as your account is active plus 3 years. Application records are retained for 5 years for legal compliance. You may request deletion of your data at any time, subject to legal retention requirements.

6. Your Rights

Under the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019, you have the right to: access your data; correct inaccurate data; request erasure; object to processing; and lodge a complaint with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, password hashing, and access controls. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Contact

Data protection queries: hirebridge@gmail.com
