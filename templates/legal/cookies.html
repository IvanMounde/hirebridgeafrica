What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. We use them to keep you signed in, remember your preferences, and understand how our Platform is used.

Cookies We Use

Essential cookies — required for the Platform to function (session management, CSRF protection). Cannot be disabled.
Preference cookies — remember your settings (e.g., consent banner dismissed).
Analytics cookies — anonymous usage statistics to help us improve the Platform.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies will impair Platform functionality. For more information visit allaboutcookies.org.
